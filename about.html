About Me

I love what I do

Hi! Thank you for stopping by.  My name is Kurt and I hope I can be of service to you.  Let me tell you a little about myself:

A Colorado native, I grew up as an army brat in the 80's and got a taste of world travel.  In the 90's I figured out I love animals and got a degree in zoology and I've never worked in a day in that field since.  Instead, being the '90's, I discovered computers and the fledgling internet and got my first instroduction to HTML and web design.

"I love doing this stuff! The more and more I work with Webflow, the more I fall in love with web design."

I discovered I was very talented with computers and proceeded to spend 15 years doing IT end-user support.  I was the geek who came to your desk when something on your computer didn't work.  During this time I created a couple web tools to help me do my job better.  I got more familar with coding, but never took it to the next step.

As skilled as I was in my IT support career, it was not fulfilling and I needed a big change away from the office.  So I did what any reasonable person would do and I became a semi truck driver. Being a truck driver has a way of getting in your blood.  I love driving and maneauvering the big rigs.  It has been a fun ride with its ups and downs.  Now the time has come for me to permanently park my rig and finally pursuing what I love - seeing a bright happy smile on someone I was able to fulfill their needs and build a relationship of trust in the process

First and foremost, I am interested in developing professional relationships with my clients.  I provide transparency, honesty, dedication, and a strong work-ethic and create the most satisfying experience for my clients. My goal is to be more than just a web developer for you, but rather a friend you can trust.

If this interests you and would like to see what I can do for you, please contact me and let's work together.
